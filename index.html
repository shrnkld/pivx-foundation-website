About

The PIVX Foundation

The world, and specifically the blockchain sector, is brimming with innovators whose ideas can shape our region and change the world for good. Specific to PIVX, we have found some of the brightest, most passionate individuals in the blockchain/cryptocurrency space. At the PIVX Foundation, we are here to support these bright individuals and give them the tools to best support their success, and through the lens of the Sustainable Development Goals, help bring PIVX’s pioneering tech to the world to be used for humanities continued betterment.

We hold ourselves to the same high standard of the talent we seek to cultivate, carrying the same ethos of PIVX itself – one that gives power and rights back to the individual. We are a small team, passionate about our work in these cutting edge realms of innovation.

The PIVX Foundation was launched in 2019, as a sub-fund of the SDG Impact Fund – which is a Public Charity Donor Advised Fund (DAF), and the only DAF in the United States that:
- Is independent of a bank
- Accepts cryptocurrency
- Is focused on helping humanity attain the SDGs

SDG Impact Fund

What is the SDG Impact Fund?

The SDG Impact Fund develops sustainable financial resources to fulfill its mission, and to ensure the continuity of charitable organizations supported by our donors and their families.

The SDGIF develops and manages endowments, planned giving, private-label DAFs,  and donor advised philanthropic funds. Our grants are provided for innovative programs, domestic/international development, domestic and foreign crisis, and securing the operational activities of charitable organizations for generations to come.
